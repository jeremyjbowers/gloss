Gloss

What is Gloss?

Gloss is a tool for annotating pictures—and letting your community contribute too.

Gloss allows users to select a region of a photo and comment on it, or to browse through other people's comments. Gloss aims to make it easy for photo editors and community members to point out interesting details on a picture, for people on any type of device to see these details, and for people to come together and have a conversation around detailed photos.

Swipe or drag on an image to make a new annotation.

Features

  • Save notes for details in an image (with normalized coordinates)
  • Notes are stored in teh cloudz (Parse)
  • Designed to be embedded in news stories
